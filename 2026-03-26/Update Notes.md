Major Update Notes - March 26, 2026

Hello, Vault Hunters!

With this Major Update, we are setting live the first Story Pack: Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned* and releasing our new Vault Hunter, C4SH the Rogue. Additionally, we’ve added some quality-of-life features, such as Shared Character Progression, and we've increased the max character level to 60.

This Major Update is approximately 20GB and all players will need to download it.

Thanks to improvements in recent versions of NVIDIA drivers, we no longer clear the game's GPU driver shader cache on first launch after updating to a new game version, when using NVIDIA GPU drivers version 590.xx or newer.

New Vault Hunter:
C4SH the Rogue

C4SH is a CasinoBot with a new lease on life. After winning a high-stakes card game, he gained a magical deck that imbues him with eldritch boons. This would be a cursed trophy for most people, but for a robot who has been able to game the solar system’s casinos and battlefields with statistical knowledge, he finally knows what unpredictability feels like. For the first time, he feels human. This drives him to play nothing safe, always bet on himself, and take as many risks as possible.

As the first of two new Vault Hunters coming to Borderlands 4 via paid Story Packs, C4SH is playable across the entire game. You can experience everything on Kairos using C4SH's unique set of skills, all while hearing how fascinated he is by anything dependent on chance!

    Added support for C4SH the Rogue

Free Content:

The following updates are free to all Borderlands 4 players, regardless of whether they own Story Pack 1 or not:

    The Level Cap has been increased 10 levels, to a max level of 60
        This long-awaited increase has finally arrived. We’re looking forward to seeing how you take advantage of these additional levels across characters. 
    Shared Character Progression
        Collectibles, Activities, Big Map Progress, SDU tokens, Vault Powers, Vehicle skins and Hover Drives are all part of the Shared Character Progression available in this update.
        No action is needed for this, and it automatically applies to all of your characters! If you’ve found different collectibles on different characters, those will all combine. 
        In Multiplayer, players will only have the map discovered for the areas they themselves have explored.
        Deleting all character saves does not reset progress. After deleting all previous characters, a new character will still have all previous map progress.
        Note that revealed Fast Travel locations still need campaign progress to take advantage of them.
