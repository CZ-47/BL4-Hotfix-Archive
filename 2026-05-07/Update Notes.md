Weekly Update Notes - May 7, 2026


Gear Updates

Dev Note: In the weeks to come before our second raid encounter, we’re going to be introducing some gear tuning and also some gear changes. The changes we can include with Weekly Updates will contain the numerical tuning passes, while the larger updates (such as the QOL update and the Raid Boss 2 update) can contain code changes for our gear systems. Starting off this week is a tuning pass to some of our DLC gear, our Pistols and SMGs, and the Order Manufacturer. 

While rapid-fire weapons have benefitted greatly from synergistic Vault Hunter passives, we wanted to make sure our hard-hitting weapons also got some love. We’re starting with a few of the Legendaries that exist in the mid-fire-rate range, then moving to other archetypes like DoTs and Splash Damage.

A note for the future: We have a change coming in the Version 1.7 Update that will cap the damage increase on Torgue Stickies to 200% bonus. Builds that infinitely regenerate ammo could stack the Torgue sticky bonus way beyond the intended means.

A note on percentages: we’ve used percentages below when the adjusted numbers don’t match up with what you see on item cards due to scaling. For example, a Shield might have had its capacity changed from 0.9 to 1.1, resulting in a ~22% increase. This “1.1” value is not shared on the item card, but we want to share what you can expect to see on changed gear (a ~22% difference to the number).

Shields:

Dev Note: Energy Shield Legendary Behaviors got a tuning pass to provide more options for players without sacrificing damage output. In addition, we felt some Legendary Energy Shields could use a capacity increase to match the damage reduction gained from Legendary Armor Shields. 

    Ripper Firebreak: Elemental Nova damage increased by 266% and shield capacity increased by 33%
    Daedalus Angel: Shield capacity increased by ~86%
    Ripper Sparky Shield: Shock damage increased by 400% and shield capacity increased by 20%
    Maliwan Pandoran Memento: Elemental Resistance increased to +25% and shield capacity increased by 30%
    Maliwan Extra Medium: Nova Elemental Splash Damage increased by 100% and shield capacity increased by 30%
    Order Cindershelly: Increased the amount of damage per stack of Resolve by 25%
    Ripper Watts 4 Dinner: Decreased the Overshield drop chance to 8% and increased the shield capacity by ~22%
    Order Protean Cell: Shield capacity has increased by 33% and the regeneration scaling has increased by 33%
