Minor Update Notes - April 2, 2026

This Minor Update includes an update to C4SH's Vampiric Touch Card, Nightmare Rift Boss activities being visible on the map, and a new set of Weekly Activities.  Change Notes:

    C4SH, the Rogue - Updated the Vampiric Touch Card granted by the Bet on Red Augment to automatically kill non-Badass enemies on impact as intended
    Nightmare Rift Bosses are now visible on the ECHO menu map to help you spot those activities! 
    Addressed a reported crash that could occur when loading into specific areas of The Whispering Glacier
    Addressed variables in some languages on C4SH's skill tree to make them easier to read
