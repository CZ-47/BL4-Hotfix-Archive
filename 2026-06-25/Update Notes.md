Version 1.8 - June 25, 2026

With today's Version 1.8 update, we're releasing new paid content with Bounty Pack 3: A Zane to Kill For*, as well as additional free content for all Borderlands 4 players! This update includes the brand new endgame raid Takedown at Hadron Abyss**, new mobbing challenges in Circle of Slaughter, Cross-Platform Saves, and additional gameplay enhancements. 

Be sure to check out our article detailing the amped-up return of the Circle of Slaughter mode, as well as the new loot and challenge modes you can find in Takedown at Hadron Abyss.

Please restart your game to ensure the update goes through and to prevent connection errors in matchmaking.

If you encounter issues, please contact 2K Support for direct assistance. You can also read the PC Troubleshooting Guide, a list of known issues, or browse a list of Support threads here.

How to start Bounty Pack 3: A Zane to Kill For

Bounty Pack 3 arrives in two distinct parts that you can purchase individually: the new narrative content and gear of A Zane to Kill For, and the new cosmetics and gear of Vault Card 3. If you own Borderlands 4 Deluxe Edition, Super Deluxe Edition, or the Bounty Pack Bundle, you already own both parts of Bounty Pack 3 (A Zane to Kill For and Vault Card 3).

If you own either part of Bounty Pack 3 on PlayStation 5, XBOX Series X|S, or PC via Epic, you'll need to navigate to both of the storefront listings for these separate parts on your platform's storefront to redeem/download the new content. You can also attempt to start the A Zane to Kill For mission (details below), which will open a direct link to redeem/download your owned Bounty Pack 3 content on your platform. If you own either part of Bounty Pack 3 on PC via Steam, this new content will be redeemed and downloaded automatically.

Once you own the Bounty Pack 3: A Zane to Kill For narrative content, you can start the mission by interacting with the arcade token on a Bounty Board.You’ll need to finish the "Wrath of the Ripper Queen" main mission so you can visit Moxxi's Bottoms Up! bar in Carcadia Burn. Once you've unlocked access to the bar, you can find the mission-starting token on any Bounty Board in a major hub area, which will be marked on your ECHO map as a new mission.

Takedown at Hadron Abyss**

Takedown at Hadron Abyss is here and is free for all Borderlands 4 players! This is the ultimate endgame challenge as you fight your way through an underwater research station filled with mutated horrors and aquatic apex predators. For the raid's final showdown, you'll have to go toe-to-fin with the Child of Terramorphous.

You can learn more about Takedown at Hadron Abyss in the reveal article and launch article!

Players must beat the main campaign before you can embark on this Takedown and try to defeat the Child of Terramorphous.  You can start the Takedown at Hadron Abyss mission via any Raid Board found in Kairos' major hub areas.

Circle of Slaughter

Circle of Slaughter mode is back, and it's free for all Borderlands 4 players! See how long you can survive against waves of increasingly deadly enemies in this mayhem-fueled gauntlet.

    How to Play: You can unlock this mode after finishing the "Wrath of the Ripper Queen" main story mission.
    Where to Find It: Head to the corner of Moxxi's Bottoms Up! bar to find Zane's arcade cabinets. Interact with the Silent Crisis: Time Scope 5 machine to teleport directly into the virtual arena.

For more Circle of Slaughter info, check out our launch article!
Cross-Platform Saves

Cross-Platform Saves are now live! This feature lets you upload your saves from one platform via SHiFT to continue playing on another platform where you own Borderlands 4. Please note that SHiFT code rewards like Golden Keys will not transfer platforms as part of Cross-Platform Saves, but will still be available on the platform you redeemed them for. 

Check out the dedicated Cross-Platform Saves Support page for full details.
